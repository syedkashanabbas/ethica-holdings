• Investment criteria

We Invest In What Deserves To Last

Because where your money goes should always make sense — to your head and your heart. We don’t jump at every opportunity. We pause, we ask the right questions, and we look for projects that feel real, grounded, and worth believing in.

It Starts With Purpose

If a project doesn’t have a clear reason to exist, we step away. We look for ideas that are trying to simplfy and make life better

We Trust People, Not Avatars

Behind every great idea is a team. We invest in people who are visible, reachable, and open about what they’re building and why

Built To Stand, Not Just Launch

We’re drawn to projects that are steady on their feet — with clean structures, strong systems, and the patience to grow the right way

Does It Add Something Good?

If it brings more fairness, access, or care into the world — we’re listening. We like ideas that help more people be part of something better

Safe Is Non-Negotiable

We don’t take chances with your trust. Every protocol we choose has to show that it’s thought about safety — and proven it

Ready for Real-World Rules

If something avoids regulation, we avoid it. We prefer teams who are building for the long run — not hiding from the rules

• Roadmap

Our Roadmap to a Kinder Financial Future

Phase 1

Discover

The foundation phase where vision meets validation. This is where Ethica comes alive — launching its first product, setting up its Swiss base, and proving its model with early believers.

  • 1. MVP launch with basic AI-powered DeFi investment features
  • 2. Swiss entity established with full regulatory setup (AML/KYC)
  • 3. First 100+ clients onboarded via personal networks
  • 4. Initial DeFi portfolio created with secure, transparent protocols
  • 5. Consultant partnerships begin (focus: Swiss & Italian advisors)

Phase 2

Build

Time to grow, improve, and expand into the world. A full year of development, education, and European scaling — laying the bricks of long-term infrastructure.

  • 1. Mobile AI investment app launched with smart contract integration
  • 2. Expansion into Germany & France under MiCA compliance
  • 3. Ethica Academy launched — education with certification
  • 4. White-label DeFi tools offered to banks
  • 5. Incubator program for ethical DeFi startups
  • 6. Strategic partnerships with financial advisors and banks
  • 7. 1,000+ active clients milestone

Phase 3

Evolve

From platform to ecosystem — a global reach begins. Ethica steps beyond Europe, begins DAO governance, and expands tech into powerful new tools.

  • 1. Expansion into Asia, MENA, and Latin America
  • 2. Quantum-resistant security and AI risk tools integrated
  • 3. Launch of DAO governance model
  • 4. Cross-chain DeFi platform activated
  • 5. Fortune 500 partnerships begin
  • 6. Growth targets: unicorn-stage valuation and full ecosystem deployment

Phase 4

Lead

Establishing leadership — ethically, technologically, globally. Now leading Europe’s ethical DeFi space, Ethica focuses on deeper innovation and institutional solutions.

  • 1. Market leader in EU for ethical DeFi
  • 2. Quantum computing integrated into investment algorithms
  • 3. Cross-chain automated yield optimization live
  • 4. Institutional-grade custody and DeFi solutions launched
  • 5. Expansion into Africa and Southeast Asia
  • 6. IPO or strategic acquisition planning
  • 7. Full ESG & sustainable finance model deployed

Phase 5

Connect

Bridging systems, governments, and the human side of AI. From central banks to next-gen AI advisors, this is where Ethica becomes an industry pillar.

  • 1. Launch of Ethica Global Network (regional HQs)
  • 2. Partnerships with central banks for CBDC integration
  • 3. Blockchain-based identity system introduced
  • 4. Next-gen AI financial assistant (human-collaborative) goes live
  • 5. Venture Capital arm launched for early-stage investing
  • 6. B-Corp certification for social impact

Phase 6

Flourish

Vision realized. Now building a legacy that lasts. Ethica achieves its €100M goal, goes fully decentralized, and begins exploring the next frontier — space, education, and global impact.

  • 1. €100M+ revenue target reached
  • 2. Full DAO governance in place
  • 3. Launch of sustainable finance platform
  • 4. Educational institutions opened in multiple countries
  • 5. Space-based financial research begins
  • 6. Global impact report published
